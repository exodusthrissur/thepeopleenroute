Emissaries of Hope.

    We are all on a journey - a journey within and without. But when the going becomes rough and the night dark, we sometimes need someone to hold us up, reminding us of the New Dawn approaching. 
    Those of us who have seen the world running backwards in our generation, and people yearning after things that would get themselves destroyed, know very well that there is a void left in each of us that we all long to fill. And finding just that in our very own journey, we, The People Enroute, envision to pass it on to others ― to help ourselves see the world better in a new light, and to breathe in the fresh air of Hope.
